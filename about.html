Why Books?

Some people prefer tangible reference materials. For example, travelers can create custom travel guides, students may want to annotate physical copies, and language learners can practice with limitless volumes of any topic.

Limitations

4-800 pages. Books under 32 pages are printed as saddleback, which is typically a bit more expensive. For the best value, it's encouraged to combine articles into single books.
